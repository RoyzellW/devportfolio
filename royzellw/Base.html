What is a Software Developer?

A software Developer is a person who commits themselves to a life of learning and adapting to the wave of coming technological revolutions. They are a person who has decided that one way of operating and perceiving is no longer beneficial. Code is always changing and becoming more and more advanced; and thus; a developer is a person who has decided that they too will always be open to change.
