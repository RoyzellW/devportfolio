Who am I?

I am an aspiring Full-Stack Software Developer within the CodeCrew Code School program. I have spent the last few months learning and aspiring to master all of the techniques that I have come across. Outside of my life as a coder I am an active rock climber and lover of meditation. I have found that rock climbing is similar to coding as they both require perseverance and the ability to look at problems from different angles. A rock climbing course has a set route with many alternate harder (and sometimes easier) routes, and so to does coding. One objective can be done in many different ways. In other words to sum up who I am, I am a student- always in the mind set to learn.

What is a Software Developer?

A software Developer is a person who commits him or herself to a life of learning and adapting to the wave of coming technological revolutions. He or she is a person who has decided that one way of operating and perceiving is no longer beneficial. Code is always changing and becoming more and more advanced; and thus, a developer is a person who has decided that they too will always be open to change.

What's next?

It was once said to me, "A man without a vision is a man without a future." I believe that to be true in every sense of the word. My vision is to be able to advance high enough within the Tech field; not just so that I may better myself, but also so that I may be able to help others in the near and far future find there way as well. I plan to give back to my community by aiding others in taking their first steps into the world and the life of a software developer.
